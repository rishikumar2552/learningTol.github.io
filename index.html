MASS COMMUNICATION

Mass communication disperses information to a huge number of people, i.e., the whole country or the world, often in real time. Media assumes greater significance because of its role in mediating cultures, influencing national and international affairs. Our course prepares the individual for this content and communication dominated world where it is almost impossible to ignore the infoquake and opportunities of today.

HOTEL MANAGEMENT

Hospitality management is the study of the hospitality industry. A degree in the subject may be awarded either by a university college dedicated to the studies of hospitality management or a business school with a relevant department. Degrees in hospitality management may also be referred to as hotel management, hotel and tourism management, or hotel administration.
