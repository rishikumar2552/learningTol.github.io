Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University – IPU CET

The GGSIPU (Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University) has been conducting the IPU CET entrance examination to complete the admission process for various courses that are offered by the university. The admission to any programme of studies within GGSIPU is done based upon the marks and ranks scored by a candidate in this particular CET (Common Entrance Test) that are conducted by the university.

Details of Exam

The Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University has released the official notification regarding the application process and examination date for the entrance examination.

Important Dates

Commencement of online application – Second week of February 2018

Last date for application – 1st May 2018

Admit card Release – Second week of May 2018

IPU CET 2018 Exam date and time – 13th May 2018 (2 PM to 4:30 PM)

IPU CET 2018 Result announcement – 24th May 2018

Counselling starts from – Last week of May 2018 or first week of June 2018.

Eligibility Criteria

In order to become an eligible candidate to appear for IPU CET examination, the aspirants must meet up with below-mentioned eligibility criteria:

  • • Candidates must have completed and passed their class 12 examination from a recognized state or central board, or with any other equivalent examination.
  • • The aspirants must have obtained about fifty five percentage of marks in subjects namely, Chemistry, Physics and in Mathematics and must have passed in them separately.
  • • The candidate must have completed and passed in English language, either as an elective subject or as a functional one.
  • • The students who are at present appearing for their class 12 board examinations in this academic year are also eligible to apply for this entrance examination.
Exam Pattern
  • • Mode: Offline
  • • Question Type: Objective Type – Multiple Choice
  • • Language: Bilingual (Hindi and English)
  • • Total No. of Marks: 600
  • • Total No. of Questions: 150
  • • Duration: 190 minutes
  • • Marking Scheme: 4 mark for every correct answer
  • • Negative marking: 1 mark shall be deducted for the wrong answer.
Section Wise Split-up marks pattern
  • • General English – 38 questions
  • • Management Aptitude – 37 questions
  • • Logical Reasoning – 38 questions
  • • General Awareness – 37 questions
Cut Off

The IPU CET cut off is mostly determined based upon following factors.

  • • Total number of seats that are about to be filled up
  • • Total number of candidates who have appeared for the entrance examination
  • • Same number of marks obtained by different students
  • • The category to which the candidate belongs to.
Syllabus

The syllabus for IPU CET 2018 will comprise topics taken from both 11th and 12th CBSE class syllabus, namely physics, chemistry, mathematics, biology and biotechnology.

Mathematics: Numbers, Averages and percentages, simple and compound interest, ratio and proportion, algebraic formulae, time, speed and distance, pipes and cisterns, lines, angles and triangles, data interpretation etc.
